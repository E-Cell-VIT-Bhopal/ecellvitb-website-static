FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of events are organised in E-cell?

There are different club activities/events are organised under E-cell like Sell-X, Start-up Bootcamps, Investors Meet, etc. In these events, it lets you discover about your critical, analytical and entrepreneurship skills.

What is the full form of E-cell?

The E-cell stands for Entrepreneurship cell.

How many separate departments are there in E-cell?

There are around 9-10 departments, which works for the management of E-cell like Content Team, Design Team, Technical Team, etc.

How we can directly contact to the team of E-cell?

You can directly fill the contact form available on the website of E-cell. Else, you can react out through mail at ecell@vitbhopal.ac.in or contact on +91 7082439684.

How I can join or become the member of E-cell?

Being a student of VIT Bhopal, you can join the E-cell team as a Core member or Member according to your potential. For joining, the recruitment form will be available on the portal and other social platforms of E-cell, where you can apply.

What support we get from E-cell?

If you have creative ideas related to start-ups, then surely, it’s a platform for you, where you can get proper guidance and procedural help as well.

On which social platforms, we can find the accounts of E-cell?

E-cell is available on different social platforms such as Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn and GitHub.

Is E-cell a club where students are joined?

No, Entrepreneurship Cell is the main cell under which different clubs perform related to the field of start-ups.

How many total members are there in E-cell?

There are more than 80 members in the cell segmented in different niches.

What are the processes of selection in the team of E-cell?

For getting recruited in the team of E-cell, you have to first fill the recruitment form, and based on that, elimination will be there. Those who get selected will be given 2nd task and after getting selected in that, you’ll have to give interview with the Lead Member. The final results will be declared after that, and joining letters will be mailed.

Our mission at E-Cell, VIT Bhopal is to support young entrepreneurs in their endeavours, and we are dedicated to fostering an entrepreneurial environment and start-up culture on campus.
